I'm a scientific software developer and bioinformatician currently working at the University of Basel and Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics. At work, I enjoy to combine my biological background with programming skills to support biologists with state of the art tools.
In my spare time I brew beer, make sausages and play bass guitar.

I obtained my PhD in Bioinformatics from Basel University (Switzerland), and my MSc in Biotechnology from Jagiellonian University (Poland). I also hold MSc degree in Molecular Biology from Université d'Orléans (France).
